Get Involved!

Spread the Word

Tell a friend about Lark. Send a friend this site or Lark's facebook page: facebook.com/LarkForJudge

It seems like a trivial thing, but it's incredibly helpful to the campaign.

Volunteer

Volunteer your time (canvass with Lark!) or your real estate (yard signs!). Email Lark@larkforjudge.com

If you don't have time or real estate and just think Lark should canvass your neighborhood, let her know that too.

Donate

Your donation can help us reach people we don't yet know.

Here are examples of what a $20 contribution allows the committee to purchase:

  • 1 large sign
  • 8 yard signs
  • 45 mailers
  • 140 door hangers
  • You can donate to the Lark Cowart for Judge Committee via PayPal.

You must be a U.S. Citizen to donate and you must be 18 years or older.
